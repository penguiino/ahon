Our promise

The term "Ahon" is a Filipino word that means "to rise out of". It is often used figuratively to describe a process redemption and growth. This accurately describes our core Mission and Vision as an organization.

Our processes are based on the open innovation paradigm, which means that we collaborate with our clients to understand their needs, to design and implement tailor-made solutions and to measure the success of these solutions. This way, we'll know what works best for them and we'll be able to provide them with a higher quality of service.

A unique client relationship is the foundation of our success.

Professionals at work

We are a business process outsourcing company that delivers HR services globally and locally. Our team is driven by our passion for people and we set high standards of excellence.

We offer a wide range of HR services, to go with any size of company:

  • We help you recruit the best talent with our unique sourcing strategies, while taking care of the administrative work. Let our experts take care of payroll and benefits administration for you.
  • We handle all the calculations, distribution of data to your HR/Payroll system, and filing.
  • Our HR administration services make it easy for you to keep up with all the compliance requirements and ensure that contracts are signed and executed as per regulatory norms.
  • Our HR systems consulting and implementation services can set up customized HR platforms for your company.
